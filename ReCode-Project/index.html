My amazing journey in web development started with RECODE. in November 2018 I was one of those lucky enough to attend their weekend workshop, this ignited a spark in me and I became eager to continue this new path that I became fond of.

RECODE team was inspiring and focused on helping us the refugees in Malaysia to see the new opportunities available as web developers. As this was my introduction to the web development world, I didn't want to stop and seeked online courses to learn more and during those few months RECODE team kept in touch and their guidance and help was simply amazing.

While this website serves the purpose of an application to the Bootcamp provided by RECODE, this page is to show myself how much I learned and a reminder to my future-self how much I was missing still.
